SUCCESS STORY
Great Starts
For the Littlest Oregonians
Benji, strong on charm and surprisingly witty, arrived on the scene
at 2 a.m. on New Year's Day. It's our business to provide big returns
on small beginnings, so in honor of being the first baby at Salem
Hospital in 2018, we gave Benji a $529 jump on his college savings.

But, hey, early starts are awesome for everyone.

If you're expecting, or know someone who is, we've got great news:
Starting January 1, 2018, when you open an account for a kiddo
under one, you'll get a $25 boost from us. Nothing like a little
hustle out of the gate.

Oregon College Savings Plan
Melissa Ceron, the newest member of our board, is a native Oregonian and recent graduate of the Oregon Institute of Technology. She comes to us with a wealth of intelligence and a passion for social change and has this to say about her commitment to the Oregon College Savings Plan:

"Empowerment, inclusivity, and accessibility are a part of the mission of the Oregon College Savings Plan. One of the values that especially resonates with what I hope for the Plan is to offer opportunities inclusive of all Oregonians, especially underserved populations. My hope is that we begin to see the rise of a culture of saving across Oregon, from rural to urban areas of the state."

This is right in line with our vision for 2018.
And we're glad to welcome her to the Board.
